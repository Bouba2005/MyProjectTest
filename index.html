Commitment to Community

Taxi app technology makes good things happen by helping people move, whether across town or towards their dreams. Opportunities appear, open-up, become reality. What started as a way to tap a button to get a ride has led to billions of moments of human connection as people around New York all kinds of places in all kinds of ways.

Supporting Cities

Taxi app technology has help make New york safer, more efficient, and more beautiful. Drivers and passengers are safe than ever as their personal information is kept by taxi companies. People of New York safely hail taxi from their home or office days and nights,

Diversity and Inclusion

Because of App companies, people are treated equal. We see different perspectives which can only be achieved through diversity, inclusion, and acknowledgment. People of color are less refused than before by taxi drivers. Also, the mobility of blind people has advance considerably.

Opportunity for All

Each day, many thousands of people around New York use app taxis to work in a way that fits around their lives. Independent work through app companies can be a powerful tool for creating economic opportunity for people with different backgrounds and aspirations.
